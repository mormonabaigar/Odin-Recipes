Tortang Talong

Eggplant coated with scrambled egg

Tortang Talong is a classic recipe, a perfect go-to in most Filipino households.

Ingredients

  • Chinese Eggplant
  • 2 pieces egg
  • 1 teaspoon salk
  • 6 tablespoon cooking oil

Directions

  1. grill the Eggplant until the color of the skin turns almost black
  2. let the eggplant cool for a while then peel off the skin. Set aside.
  3. Crack the eggs and place in a bowl
  4. Add salt and beat
  5. Place eggplant on a flat surface and flatten using fork
  6. Dip the flatten eggplant in the beaten egg mixture
  7. Heat the pan and pour cooking oil
  8. Fry the eggplant (that was dipped in the beaten mixture). Make sure that
    both sides are cooked. Frying time will take about 3-4 minutes per
    side on medium heat.
